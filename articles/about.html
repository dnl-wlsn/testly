About

Testly was created as a guide for everyone - software test engineers who are new to the game (or wanting to get in to it), as well as those who have been testing for years.

Testers are so important to any organisation, but they're often considered unnecessary or 'bottlenecks'... even in !

I started as a video games tester, and have since worked in a number of industries such as finance tech, retail, estate agency, and insurance.
