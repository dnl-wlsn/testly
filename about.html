About

The reason behind the idea

The pitch

Pair Up was designed for the 2019 Simply Business Hackathon, but has a very legitimate and real world business case behind it.

We’re more connected than ever... yet depression and loneliness is on the rise. We’re forgetting how to make new connections, maintain existing friendships, and are becoming increasingly more isolated because of it.

We wanted to encourage people to reconnect and get together in real life, and not from behind a computer screen or device.

So, Pair Up was created. It aims to connect people with similar interests by using a predetermined list of social activities, mixing general, collection, competitive, and observation hobbies to ensure that there is something for everyone.

Day 1 | The plan

The MVP is:

  • A clean, appealing website to entice people to sign up
  • Sign up functionality (Name, email, phone number)
  • Ability to choose (up to) 5 hobbies
  • The list was taken from this Wikipedia page.
  • Mobile number revealed with a successful pairing
  • To have a good time whilst building it, and hopefully learn a thing or two!
  • If there are no matches, show a message along the lines of "We can't find anyone interested in [activity]. Have you thought about trying [activity]?"

    • Stretch goals:

  • Image uploader for a profile photo
  • Make the search filterable by radius (which would require a postcode, I think?)
